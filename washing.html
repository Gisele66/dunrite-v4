Where it began

Three decades of spotless results

Dunrite was founded in 1993 on a simple promise: do exceptional washing work and stand behind it. That reputation for reliability is what later allowed us to expand into moving and delivery.

From monthly storefront window cleaning to seasonal whole-property power washing, our crews bring the right equipment, technique and attention to detail to every job.
