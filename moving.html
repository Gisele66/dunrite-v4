Specialized transport

The routes others won't take

In 2020, David saw a real need for specialized transport to Haida Gwaii. In 2021 the first trailer joined the fleet — and Dunrite began moving households and hauling larger loads to and from some of BC's most remote communities.

Today we move families, deliver goods, and handle specialty pickups along the entire corridor between Prince George, the coast, and the islands — with the same care that built our washing business since 1993.
