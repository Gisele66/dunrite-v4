For over 30 years, Dunrite has earned the trust of homeowners and businesses across North Central BC — one dependable job at a time.

Our story

From a ladder and a bucket to a fleet on the road

David Paterson founded Dunrite in 1993, serving Prince George with residential and commercial window washing and power washing. Through decades of honest work, the Dunrite name became shorthand for reliability across the region.

In 2020, David recognized a real need for specialized transport to Haida Gwaii. In 2021 he added the first trailer to his fleet and began moving households and transporting larger loads of goods — extending that same dependable service to BC's most remote routes.

Today, Dunrite is a true family operation, combining two complementary services under one trusted brand.
